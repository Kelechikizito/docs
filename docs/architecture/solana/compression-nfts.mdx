## Compression NFTs Update

Seven months after the announcement of compressed NFTs, the Solana Foundation has conclusive data on the actual cost of using compression. 
As of Dececember 2023, any project(**Helium**) can use cNFTs to mint one million NFTs for $247.80 (compared to the initial estimates of $113.42 in April 2023).
